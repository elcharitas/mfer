Detect and Classify Brain-Tumor

This solution makes use of an advanced customized deep learning model, that allows the prediction of glioma, meningioma, and pituitary tumors.

How it works?

A deep learning model used to predict and classify brain tumors. The model is trained on a dataset of MRI images of the brain. It is able to predict and classify glioma, meningioma, and pituitary tumors.
In order to predict and classify brain tumors, you would need to upload MRI image(s) of the brain. The model will then analyze the image(s) and provide you with the prediction and classification of the brain tumor. If no tumor is detected, the model will provide you with a negative result.
